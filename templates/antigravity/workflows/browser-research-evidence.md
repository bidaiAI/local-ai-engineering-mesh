# Browser Research → Evidence Workflow

Use this workflow when the task is browser-heavy, research-heavy, or artifact-heavy.

## Goals
- keep browsing structured
- separate collection from synthesis
- preserve artifact evidence for later review

## Steps
1. restate the task and success criteria
2. collect only the minimum pages or artifacts needed
3. capture screenshots or extracted facts as evidence, not as decoration
4. summarize what was verified vs what remains uncertain
5. hand off a compact result back into the shared project runtime

## Output contract
- summary
- evidence list
- open questions
- recommended next action
